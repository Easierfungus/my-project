Return Policy

Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a 30-day return policy from the date of purchase.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unworn and unused
  • In the original packaging
  • With all tags attached
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team to initiate your return
  2. Package your item securely in the original packaging
  3. Include the completed return form (available on our website)
  4. Ship the package to our return address

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Exchanges

If you need a different size or color, please contact us to arrange an exchange. We'll cover the shipping costs for exchanges of defective or incorrect items.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as final sale
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Items without original packaging

Contact Us

For any questions about our return policy, please contact us at returns@styletees.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567.
